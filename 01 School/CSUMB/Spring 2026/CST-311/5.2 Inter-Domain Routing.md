# AS Numbers
- Each AS identified by an ASN number
	- 16-bit values (latest protocol supports 32-bit ones)
	- 64512 - 65535 are reserved
- There are more than 100,000 ASNs as of March 2021
	- AT&T: 5074, 6341, 7018, ...
	- Sprint: 1239, 1240, 6211, 6242, ...
	- CSU: 2153
	- Google: 15169, 36561 (formerly YouTube), + others
	- Facebook: 32934

# Internet Inter-AS Routing: BGP
- **BGP (Border Gateway Protocol):** The de facto inter-domain routing protocol
	- "glue that holds the Internet together"
- Allows subnet to advertise its existence, and the destinations it can reach, to the rest of Internet
	- "I am here, here is who I can reach, and how"
	- **Policy-based** routing protocol
		- Policies driven by economics
			- How much `$$$` does it cost to route along a given path?
			- Not by performance (e.g. shortest paths)
- BGP provides each AS as a means to:
	- **eBGP**: Obtain subnet reachability information from neighboring ASs
	- **iBGP**: Propagate reachability information to all AS-internal routers
- Determine "good" routes to other networks based on reachability information and policy
